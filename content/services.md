## Ways We Can Work Together

Every client’s situation is different, so services are flexible and tailored to individual needs.

### One-on-One Guided Sessions

In-home or virtual sessions focused on reviewing belongings, organizing spaces, and supporting decision-making.

These sessions provide structure, accountability, and a calm environment for working through areas that feel overwhelming.

### Life Transitions

Support during major changes such as:

- Downsizing
- Preparing for a move
- Transitioning after the loss of a loved one
- Helping parents or family members reduce belongings
- Adjusting homes to reflect a new phase of life

### Ongoing Support

Some clients prefer to work gradually over time. Ongoing sessions allow for thoughtful progress without pressure.
