## About Ashley

Creating Space Consulting was founded on a simple belief: the way we relate to our belongings often reflects deeper life transitions.

For many people, letting go of objects is not simply about tidying or organizing. It involves memory, identity, family history, and sometimes grief.

My role is not to impose a system or push decisions. It is to create a calm environment where clients can thoughtfully move through these moments with support.

I work with individuals navigating a wide range of circumstances—from major life transitions to the gradual accumulation that happens over many years.

Clients often say that what they value most is having a neutral, compassionate presence who helps make the process feel manageable.

## My Approach

This work is guided by a few simple principles:

- **Respect for personal history** – belongings often represent meaningful chapters of life
- **Pacing that feels manageable** – decisions are never rushed
- **Practical structure** – gentle guidance that helps prevent overwhelm
- **Judgment-free collaboration** – every home and situation is different

The goal is not perfection.
It is clarity, comfort, and a home that supports the life you are living now.
